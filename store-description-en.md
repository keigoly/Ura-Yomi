# Chrome Web Store Description (English)

---

--- What is Ura-Yomi!? ---

A Chrome extension that analyzes YouTube video comments using the power of Google Gemini AI.
Instantly analyze thousands of comments to reveal what viewers really think.


--- What It Can Do ---

[AI Analysis]
- Overall Summary: AI-generated natural language summary of comment trends
- Sentiment Analysis: Positive / Neutral / Negative ratios displayed as a pie chart
- Topic Extraction: Automatically identifies trending discussion topics
- Deep Dive: Gemini selects representative comments for each sentiment and explains why

[Hidden Gems]
- AI discovers valuable comments that have low like counts

[Comment Viewer]
- YouTube-style thread view
- Sort by popularity, likes, or date
- Real-time search
- Natural time display ("3 days ago", "2 months ago")


--- Supported Content ---

- Regular videos (/watch?v=...)
- YouTube Shorts (/shorts/...)
- Paste any URL to start analysis
- Analyze up to 10,000 comments at once


--- Character Mode ---

Turn it ON to have two original characters present the analysis results in their unique speaking styles.
Enjoy an entertaining twist on the standard analysis output.


--- Customization ---

- Multilingual: Japanese / English (full UI + AI analysis results)
- Themes: Light / Dark Blue / Black
- Font Size: 5 adjustable levels
- Analysis History: Save up to 30 past results
- Import/Export Settings: Backup in JSON format


--- How to Use (3 Easy Steps) ---

1. Authenticate with your Google account
2. Open any YouTube video
3. Click the analyze button!

No API key setup required.


--- Credit System ---

- 3 credits per analysis
- Free credits on first sign-up
- Purchase additional credits from the settings page


--- Privacy ---

- API keys are managed server-side (never exposed to users)
- Analysis history is stored in your browser (localStorage)
- All communication is HTTPS encrypted
- Only the minimum necessary data is collected
